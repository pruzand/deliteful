Getting Help

Checkout our Users Forum below. If you found an issue in our code, don't hesitate to report it on our issues list with as much details as possible to help us solving it out.

Contributing

We definitely welcome contributions. All contributions require signing the Dojo Foundation CLA, once done, you can go ahead fork & hack our repo from github and then submit your pull request. Checkout our contribution guidelines for more details

You can also reach us out on the developers group to discuss prior to your contribution.
